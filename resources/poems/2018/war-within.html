A war within

He’s fighting a war, a war within
Defending a castle that never belonged to him
The folk look happy unaware of the winds,
The darkness, though, calmly rages within.


The queen is gone and so are the knights
Leaving him alone to cry in these fights
Its not his army nor his throne
He has to fight this enemy all alone.


All is not sweet, All is not sound,
Weeps the king, for someone to know.
Still, he fights
And brings back the light.


Smiles the darkness and laughs at him,
He was the one, who had let him win.


Seeing the shadow rise,
From deep down within,
the king only said…
I am tired of fighting this war, this war within
trying to defend the castle, the lost castle within…
